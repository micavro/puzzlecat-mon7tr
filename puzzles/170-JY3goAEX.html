Observations

  • The English text is: THlS lS my FIRST puzzIe. You wanna soIve IT? TelI lRIS the number of I.
  • The puzzle deliberately mixes uppercase I and lowercase l, which are visual homoglyphs in many fonts.
  • Flavor: 你看到了我，你看到的我……
  • Wrong answers have no cooldown.

Reasoning

Counting actual uppercase I characters in the English puzzle sentence gives seven:

  1. FIRST
  2. puzzIe
  3. soIve
  4. IT
  5. TelI
  6. the internal I in lRIS
  7. the final standalone I

The apparent I-like glyphs in THlS, lS, and the first character of lRIS are lowercase l, so they are decoys.

Submitting 7 produced the custom milestone 好像漏了哪里……, confirming that the sentence-only count is intended but incomplete. The title 我解谜 I puzzIe. contributes two additional uppercase I characters: the standalone I and the substituted I in puzzIe. The complete count is therefore 7 + 2 = 9.

Candidate ranking

  1. 9 - full-page count after the milestone explicitly indicates an omitted location.
  2. 7 - sentence-only count, confirmed as an intermediate.
  3. 10 - counts both uppercase I and lowercase l in the sentence, contradicting the request for actual I characters.

Submissions

  • 7 -> intermediate: 好像漏了哪里…….
  • 9 -> correct.

Final answer

9
