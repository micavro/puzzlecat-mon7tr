Solve log: ！？土土？！ (1wsaRpKD)

Owner: recover-w1-20260712

Mechanism

Each letter A-H stands for one Chinese character. The clues determine:

Letter Character Evidence
A 地 A is a character with the B radical; later clues place A under feet and contain a metro.
B 土 地 has 土 as its side/radical; 土地 is mostly made of soil. This is the letter explicitly representing “土”.
C 铁 AC = 地铁, a vehicle.
D 路 A similar vehicle runs on CD = 铁路; there can be roads above the ground.
E 泥 EB = 泥土, another expression involving soil.
F 下 AF = 地下 contains a metro; FB = 下土 occurs in “汗滴禾下土”.
G 脚 GF = 脚下, where the ground is; feet frequently touch the ground, and muddy ground can make a foot sink.
H 山 Mountains are formed by movement of the earth and are a great mass of soil; HG = 山脚 and HF = 山下 have approximately the same meaning.

Substitute into the requested final string:

HGF的EBD和A里的C

becomes:

山脚下的泥土路和地里的铁

This is the requested grammatical sentence.

Submissions

  • 山脚下的泥土路和地里的铁 - correct (attempt 1).
