Rules and image

Each mini-puzzle has three clues corresponding in some order to the target's sound, form, and meaning. V plays a different one of those roles in each mini-puzzle. English mini-answers may be submitted for validation.

The final image says the three answers index a table. For answer one, its ordinal has tens digit alpha and units digit beta. It constrains the final answer's height, length, number of letters, and a relation to answer three.

Mini-puzzles

  1. [V] [烦] [银灰]: VANADIUM. V is its chemical symbol, 烦 approximates its opening sound, and vanadium is a silver-gray metal. The site confirmed this mini-answer.
  2. [V] [CARROT] [符号]: CARET. It sounds like carrot, is a symbol, and the caret glyph has the form of an inverted V. This is very strong.
  3. [V] [是] [底部]: FOOT, confirmed by the site. A foot is a bottom; the bottom component/form of 是 is 疋, historically/graphically associated with a foot; V supplies the remaining loose semantic association through notation/unit ideas.

Final extraction

VANADIUM has atomic number 23 in the periodic table, hence alpha=2 and beta=3. CARET means ^, so the image gives:

  • Height: 2^3 FOOT = 8 feet.
  • Length: 2^3 x 3 FOOT = 24 feet.
  • Letter count: 2^2 = 4.
  • The answer is related to FOOT.

A regulation soccer goal is exactly 8 feet high and 24 feet wide. GOAL has four letters and is directly related to football, satisfying every constraint.
