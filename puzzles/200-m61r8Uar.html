Observations

  • The puzzle is a single black image containing five rows of white shapes, with flavor 字字孔明诸格亮.
  • It is an unused draft of P&KU2's 月照纱窗 and explicitly has the same mechanism but a different answer.
  • The public moonlight_window generator and the local P&KU2 solution confirm the mechanism: the white shapes are only the enclosed regions (holes/counters) of Chinese characters. Characters without enclosed regions disappear completely.
  • This early draft format consists of five independent four-character idioms. Each row has one invisible character.

Reasoning

The visible counter shapes were matched against the generator's common-character dictionary. A 30,895-entry idiom corpus then gave an unambiguous best match for every row:

  1. 貌合神离: 离 has no enclosed region and is invisible.
  2. 家喻户晓: 家 has no enclosed region and is invisible.
  3. 博古通今: 今 has no enclosed region and is invisible.
  4. 曾几何时: 几 has no enclosed region and is invisible.
  5. 口碑载道: 载 has no enclosed region and is invisible.

Reading the missing characters from top to bottom gives 离家今几载.

Submissions

  • 离家今几载 -> correct.
  • A later command issued while checking a delayed tool response detected alreadySolved: true and did not submit again.

Final answer

离家今几载
