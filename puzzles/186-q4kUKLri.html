Solve log: 料理 (q4kUKLri)

Owner: recover-w1-20260712

Mechanism

The icons are the standard Japanese cooking seasonings from the さしすせそ mnemonic, and a number on an icon selects a kana from the ingredient's Japanese name.

  • Sugar sack = 砂糖 (さとう). Positions 2 and 3 are と and う; adding the shown ふ gives とうふ -> 豆腐.
  • Salt shaker = 塩 (しお). Position 2 is お; vinegar = 酢 (す); adding し gives おすし -> お寿司.
  • The soy-sauce row and its historical reading せうゆ reinforce the cooking-seasoning / kana theme.
  • Miso bowl = 味噌 (みそ). Position 2 is そ; adding ば gives そば -> 蕎麦.

The final row asks for position 1 from sugar, salt, and miso:

  • 砂糖 position 1 = さ
  • 塩 position 1 = し
  • 味噌 position 1 = み

Together: さしみ, written 刺身.

Candidate ranking

  1. 刺身 - exact kana extraction and expected two-kanji dish.
  2. さしみ - equivalent kana spelling, but the examples present final dishes in kanji, so 刺身 is preferred.

Submissions

  • 刺身 - correct (attempt 1).

Incorrect count: 0.
