  • Title: 成语接龙
  • Pattern: ABCD -> DAEF -> FGEA -> ABCD
  • Asked answer: ABCD

Reasoning

The source clue for DAEF is 《庄子·天道》. The well-known idiom derived from the wheelwright passage is 得心应手, so:

  • D=得
  • A=心
  • E=应
  • F=手

Then FGEA has the form 手?应心. 手不应心 is an idiom from 《警世通言》第三十二卷, fixing G=不 and confirming the prior letters.

The two-character clue AD is therefore 心得, matching the stated early occurrence in 《吕氏春秋·先己》. 《墨子·亲士》 contains “非无安居也，我无安心也”, so BA=安心 and B=安.

The remaining four-character idiom is ABCD = 心安理得, giving C=理. The full loop is:

心安理得 -> 得心应手 -> 手不应心 -> 心安理得

Each next idiom begins with the previous idiom's final character.

Candidate ranking

  1. 心安理得 - uniquely satisfies all letter identities and all four source clues.

Submissions

  • 心满意得 - incorrect; based on a mistaken identification of BA as 满心. Incorrect count: 1.
  • 心安理得 - correct. Final incorrect count: 1.

Stopping criteria

Stopped on the correct verdict for 心安理得.
