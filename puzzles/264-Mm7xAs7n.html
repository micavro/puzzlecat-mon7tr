Statement

Flavor:

Tomato plants sender? 空是这么填的吗？错了！你真的该多读点书！

The final 书 is bold. The image is a 6-by-3 letter grid. Seven cells are boxed and have handwritten blue letters crossed out in red.

Mechanism

The printed and handwritten letters, read down the columns, give:

TOMATO
PLANTS
SENDER

This is the deliberately incorrect reading advertised by the English flavor and title.

The unchanged printed letters instead force a famous book title when the grid is read normally across rows:

T H E
O L D
M A N
A N D
T H E
S E A

Thus the corrected boxed letters are H, E, D, H, S, E, A, and the complete answer is THE OLD MAN AND THE SEA by Ernest Hemingway. This directly explains the bold instruction to read more 书 (books).

Candidate ranking

  1. THE OLD MAN AND THE SEA - exact text formed by the corrected grid; strongest answer.
  2. 老人与海 - standard Chinese title, plausible normalized-language alternative but not literally present in the grid.
