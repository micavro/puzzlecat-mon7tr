  • The image asks which middle school is represented.

Solution

The four lightly printed quadrant characters are:

  • upper left: 北
  • upper right: 昌
  • lower left: 南
  • lower right: 京

The colored answer markers first select the red and green quadrants, giving 北 and 京. The adjacent orange and purple half-width markers combine the relevant parts of 昌 and 南 into 明. Appending the supplied 诚学校 produces 北京明诚学校.

Submission

  • 北京明诚学校 - correct (2026-07-13 03:20 China time).
