  • A, B, and C are near-synonyms; A and C commonly describe soda.
  • Characters 1 and 2 are homophones and visually similar.
  • The question asks for the soda spokesperson who performed the role named by C2.

Reasoning

  • A1 = 冰峰, the well-known Xi'an soda brand. Thus A=冰, 1=峰.

  • 2=锋 is homophonous with and visually similar to 峰.
  • Therefore C2 = 冷锋, the protagonist of the Wolf Warrior films.
