  • Riddle: tree（打一宝可梦曾译名）

Mechanism

The English word tree literally means 树.

Read the situation word by word in Chinese:

  • 胡：外国的、外来的；这里是外国文字/外国话。
  • 说：英文 tree 正在表达、说出一个意思。
  • 树：tree 的中文意思。

Thus the clue is a foreign language saying “tree”: 胡 + 说 + 树 = 胡说树.

This matches the flavor's instruction that the surface is obtained by understanding the answer character by character, rather than by a conventional named riddle pattern.

Independent fact check

The Pokémon encyclopedia entry for National Pokédex no. 185, currently 树才怪 (Sudowoodo), lists the former mainland Chinese name 胡说树, formed from 胡说 and 树. This was checked only as Pokémon name-history verification, not by searching for this puzzle or an existing solution.

Candidate ranking

  1. 胡说树 — 98% confidence. Exact character-by-character mechanism and verified former Pokémon translation.
  2. 树才怪 — under 2%. It is the current name of the same Pokémon, but the clue does not parse character by character to it, and the prompt explicitly requests a former translation.

Submit 胡说树. Expected incorrect count before submission: 0.
