  • Title: 【鼠鼠谜】动物朋友
  • Owner: chat2-root-20260712
  • Status: correct
  • Incorrect submissions: 2

Reasoning

  • The cow's 1 2 2 must be MOO, so 1 = M and 2 = O.
  • The pig's 2 + INK is OINK, confirming 2 = O.
  • The horse panel was initially misread as 3 + IGH. The archived original shows one stylized glyph after 3, apparently a lowercase g; its exact role and the intended spelling of NEIGH still need verification.
  • MONEY was submitted from the moo + neigh homophone and was rejected.
  • Treating the horse suffix as GH gave [3] = NEI; the resulting literal string MOONEI was also rejected.
  • On the original image, the horse suffix is the numeral 8, not g or GH. Thus [3] + 8 is the rebus N-eight for neigh, giving [3] = N.
  • The final boxes therefore spell M O O N exactly.

Result

  • Submitted MOON: correct.

Stopping Criteria

  • Do not resubmit a homophone until the horse glyph and flavor text produce an exact final string.
