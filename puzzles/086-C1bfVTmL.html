  • MU-ZI-MI is a Chinese-syllable approximation of Japanese Mutsumi, the given name of Crychic member 若叶睦. This explains the work-specific framing:
  • title Cry??ic supplies CH, producing the band name Crychic;

  • The title's CH is thematic identification here, not extra answer material. The body explicitly requests three two-letter pinyins (2+2+2), so appending CH would violate the stated format.
  • Checker-ready stage assembly: MUZIMI.

Checker-directed final step
